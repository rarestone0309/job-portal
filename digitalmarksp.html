How to share your resume link:

Google Drive:

  1. Go to Google Drive and upload your resume (PDF format).
  2. Right-click the uploaded file and click "Get link".
  3. Under “General access,” choose “Anyone with the link” and set it to Viewer.
  4. Click “Copy link” and paste it into the Resume Link field above.

Dropbox:

  1. Go to Dropbox and upload your resume.
  2. Hover over the uploaded file and click "Share".
  3. Click "Copy link" and ensure the link is set to Anyone with the link can view.
  4. Paste the link into the Resume Link field above.
